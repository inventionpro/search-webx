Tips

  1. Meaningful titles: not one letter or nothing, they should be at least 6 letters.
  2. Meaningful descriptions: it should have a description, the longer the better, but not too much, less than 100 letters.
  3. Some text: Having a bit of text on the site might bump it up, but not much.
  4. Color: Include a theme color, this gives a very very small bump, but still something.
  5. Favicon: Include a favicon (the image on the tab), this gives a bit more of a bump.
  6. Quality: Make a good website, we review all websites and give ⭐ and ✔️ to those we like.
